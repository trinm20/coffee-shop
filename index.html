The Annamites is a Vietnamese coffee brand inspired by the name "An Nam", a region in Indochina where Asian and European cultures intersect. The brand incorporates the image of the traditional Vietnamese conical hat, a symbol of simplicity and elegance, along with decorative motifs from the Indochina era, creating a unique brand identity that blends classic and modern elements.

Each product from The Annamites is not just a delicious cup of coffee but also a connection to Vietnamese culture. The brand aims to provide customers with a holistic experience where flavor and cultural values intertwine, fostering a sense of closeness and connection with the land of An Nam.
